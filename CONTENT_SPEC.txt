# InAmigos Foundation Website Content Specification

> This document is the single source of truth for all website content.
> 
> 
> Every piece of text, statistic, image, project description, and contact information displayed on the website must come from the official InAmigos Foundation website.
> 
> Never invent content.
> 
> Never fabricate statistics.
> 
> Never create fictional projects.
> 
> If information is unavailable on the official website, omit it rather than guessing.

# About Section

## Heading

Who We Are

## Content

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla.

The organization works to create positive social impact by empowering communities through education, environmental sustainability, women empowerment, youth development, and animal welfare.

By connecting volunteers, professionals, students, and communities, the foundation strives to build a more compassionate, inclusive, and sustainable society.

## Mission

Uniting Minds For Change

## Vision

To empower communities through sustainable social initiatives that improve lives and create lasting positive impact.

# Content Rules

Always

- Use official organization information.
- Maintain a professional tone.
- Keep paragraphs concise and readable.
- Preserve the organization's identity.

Never

- Invent statistics.
- Add fake testimonials.
- Create fictional campaigns.
- Add partner organizations that are not officially listed.
- Exaggerate achievements.

---

# Maintenance Guidelines

Before updating website content

1. Verify information from the official website.
2. Update this document first.
3. Then update the website.

This document must always remain the authoritative source for website content.

---

# Final Objective

A visitor should finish reading the website with a clear understanding of:

- What InAmigos Foundation is.
- Why the organization exists.
- Which projects it runs.
- The impact it has created.
- How they can participate through volunteering or support.

Every section should reinforce trust, authenticity, and transparency while remaining faithful to the official information published by InAmigos Foundation.
